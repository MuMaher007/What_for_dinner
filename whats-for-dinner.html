Creamy Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, and pancetta.

Extended Preparation Time

This recipe requires more than 45 minutes to prepare. Plan accordingly!

Extended Preparation Time

This recipe requires more than 45 minutes to prepare. Plan accordingly!

1 400g spaghetti pasta
2 200g pancetta or bacon, diced
3 4 large eggs
4 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
5 Black pepper to taste
1

Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.

2

Fry pancetta until crispy, then add garlic for the last minute.

3

Whisk eggs with parmesan cheese and black pepper.

4

Toss hot pasta with pancetta and remove from heat.

5

Add egg mixture quickly, using pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
